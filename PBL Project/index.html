 1. Arts Stream :
          

            The students who are weak in maths and science can take arts as the main path.
          

          There are several subjects in the arts stream such as
          


          History, English, Sanskrit

          Geography, Psychology

          Political Science, Economics

          Sociology, Philosophy, Hindi
  
    



          





 2. Commerce Stream : 

  The Commerce stream is the best stream after the 10th class in the marketing, finance, accounting, banking, and management field.
  

  Basically, commerce is the study of various trades and business activities such as buying and selling goods, services, and other valuable things.
  

  In short Commerce, the stream is all about trade, business, accounting, banking, marketing, sales, management, advertising, warehousing, etc.




  Subjects in Commerce ( Compulsory )


Economics, Business Studies, English and Accountancy





Subjects in Commerce ( Optional + Additional ) 


Information Practice, Mathematics 

Entrepreneurship, Psychology 

Computer Science, Fine Arts 

Hindi, Physical Education etc. 
 
 



          













  3. Science Stream : 

If you want to make a career in the technical field then must choose Stream as the main course after the 10th class.


Many students prefer the science stream to make a bright future.


If you are very good at mathematics and science, want to become a doctor, engineer, pilot, scientist, want to discover something new, or want to research in a technical field then you can go for the science stream.


There are main 2 groups for the students. 
 



Group A – Non-Medical ( PCM ) – Compulsory : 


Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and English. 
 



Group – Optional : 


Computer Science, Psychology, Fine Arts, Economics, Architecture, and Physical Education. 
 



Group B – Medical ( PCB ) – Compulsory : 


Biology, Physics, Chemistry, and English 
 


Group B – Optional : 


Biotechnology, Home Science, Psychology, Mathematics, Economics, and Physical Education. 
 



 
 






   4.What is a Diploma course? 

  A diploma is a certificate awarded by a recognized college or university to make clear that someone has completed an education degree or any particular study course.


The diploma course is a short-term course usually 1 year to 3 years offered by the polytechnic colleges and other institutes in the technical and non-technical fields.


They focus on theory as well as practical knowledge and skills to prepare the students to work at the industry level.



 
 




 5.What is ITI Course?


ITI course is the courses in which students are trained to work at the industrial level.


ITI prepares students to work in industries and also makes them self-employed.


They focus more on practical knowledge and skills instead of theory.


ITI Courses may prove to be the best for those who want to job within 2 to 3 years instead of studying further.
